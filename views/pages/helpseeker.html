Help & Support

To reset your password, go to the login page, click on "Forgot Password," and follow the instructions. An email will be sent to you with a link to reset your password.

Navigate to your account settings by clicking on your profile icon at the top right corner. From there, you can update your personal information, including your email, phone number, and profile picture.

If you encounter a bug, please report it using the form below or by contacting our support team directly. Include as much detail as possible to help us resolve the issue quickly.

To delete your account, please contact our support team using the form below. Keep in mind that deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.
